THE HORSE STANCE

The horse stance has been used in several martial arts such as kung fu karate and qi gong

1. Stand up back straight with your feet together.

2. Swing your toes outwards while keeping on your heels.

3. Swing your heels outwards while keeping on your toes.

4. From this stance, lower your knees, should look similar to a sumo squat.

5. Stick your arms out to your side and keep them flexed.

6. keep this position while breathing slowly through mouth or nose for at least 5 minutes.
