Exercises And Meditation

THE ROOSTER STANCE

The rooster stance comes from thai-chi. It is used for improving your balance and prevents diseases such as dementia and alzheimer, also said to help bloodflow it gives a myriad of benefits and it is beginner friendly.

1. Stand up with your back straight.

2. Keep your chin up and eyes gazing forward

2. Lift your right leg up while bending your knee

3. Keep your right leg up and knee bent while standing on your left leg

4. keep your hands relaxed by your sides.

5. hold this position for 30sec to 1min.

5. focus on breathing and try to relax.

THE HORSE STANCE

The horse stance has been used in several martial arts such as kung fu karate and qi gong. It helps with energy,posture,mental clarity and flexibility.

1. Stand up back straight with your feet together.

2. Swing your toes outwards while keeping on your heels.

3. Swing your heels outwards while keeping on your toes.

4. From this stance, lower your knees, should look similar to a sumo squat.

5. Stick your arms out to your side and keep them flexed.

6. Keep this position while breathing slowly through mouth or nose for at least 5 minutes.

7. Try to relax and breathe

MEDITATION

Meditation can give you a sense of calm, peace and balance that can benefit both your emotional well-being and your overall health. You can also use it to relax and cope with stress by refocusing your attention on something calming. Meditation can help you learn to stay centered and keep inner peace.

1. listen to the sound of the rain.

2. breathe in for 5 seconds fill belly with air.

3. breathe out for 10 seconds push air out slowly.

4. do this for 5mins or more.

5. relax and be present.
